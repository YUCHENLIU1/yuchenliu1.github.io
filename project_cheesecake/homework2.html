How to make a baked Cheesecake

Ingredient

Crust

  • 2 Cups of Graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • Pinch salt
  • 70g Unsalted butter, melted

Filling

  • 950g cream cheese
  • 1 270g gtanulated sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoonteaspoons of vanilla
  • 4 large eggs
  • 170ml sour cream
  • 170ml heavy whipping cream

Part one

Step one

You will need to have a Springform pan because it will ensure that your cheesscake comes out of your pan smoothly

Step two

You will need to cover the pan with aluminum foil

Step three

Preheat the oven to 350 Fahrenheit

Step four

Pour the graham cracker dust into a large bowl

Use a tablespoon or a spatula to stir it in the salt and sugar. Then add melted butter into it and keep stir it untill all of the ingredients mixed well.

Hint:

You can also use your hands to mix the ingredients, but please make sure you wash your hands before mix it.

Step five

Pour the crust mixture into the pan

Press the crust down with the spatula or with your washed hands Please makeing sure there is no holes.

Place the pan in the oven

Put it in the oven for 10 minutes and remove the pan from the oven

Then turn the oven temperature down to 325 Fahrenheit

Part two

Step one

Cut your cream cheesse into large chunks and add the sugar, vanilla and salt to the cream cheese while using handheld electric mixer to stir it up for 10 minute. The result should be a smooth texture.

Step two

Add egg while the mixer is on, mix it for one minutes and repeat the process. Use the spatula to scrape along the insides and bottom of the mixing bowl

Untill all the ingredients are all blended together well.

You can use your spatula to do it

Step three

Pour the filling into the pan on the top of the crust

Step four

Put it into the lower rack of oven

You need to put it in the oven for one and half hour

Step Five

Take it out of your oven and carefully take the cake out.

Then, please carefully take out your cake and enjoy the cheesscakeyummy!.
